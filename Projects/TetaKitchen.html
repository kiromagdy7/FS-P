Teta's Kitchen

“Teta's Kitchen” Is A Full-Stack digital platform designed to preserve, organize,
And share traditional family recipes in a simple and elegant way.
It allows users to document their favorite dishes along with the stories and memories behind them,
creating a living archive of family heritage that can be accessed anytime.
The platform provides a smooth and fast user experience across all devices,
Enabling effortless browsing and management of recipes from desktop, tablet, or mobile,
while ensuring that all data is stored securely and reliably..
